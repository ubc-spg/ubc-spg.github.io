UBC Security & Privacy Group


We are a diverse group of people working on topics related to systems security, cloud security, network security, privacy, ML, and software engineering.

We are always looking to hire talented and motivated students.

Multiple Graduate Student Openings

UBC Security & Privacy Group is looking for multiple graduate students to start in the fall of 2026. Interested students must apply through the university. We are particularly interested by students working on the following topics:

  • Fuzzing (potential supervisor: Caroline Lemieux)
  • Network Security, Online Privacy, and Internet Measurement (potential supervisor: Nguyen Phong Hoang)
  • Intrusion Detection Systems (potential supervisor: Thomas Pasquier)
  • ML Security & Privacy (potential supervisor: Mathias Lécuyer)
  • Operating Systems Security (potential supervisor: Aastha Mehta, Thomas Pasquier)
  • Safe Kernel Extensions (potential supervisor: Thomas Pasquier)
  • Side Channel Counter Measures (potential supervisor: Aastha Mehta)
